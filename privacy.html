Privacy Policy — Rugby Nations Tracker

Last updated 5 July 2026

Rugby Nations Tracker does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account and contains no advertising or analytics software.

Match data

When you use the app, match fixtures, results and standings are fetched from our server, which retrieves them from third-party sports data providers. Standard technical information (such as your device's IP address) is processed transiently to deliver this data and is not logged or used to identify you.

Notifications

If a future version offers score notifications and you enable them, an anonymous device token will be used solely to deliver those alerts. It will not be linked to your identity. The current version sends no notifications.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this address with a new "last updated" date.
